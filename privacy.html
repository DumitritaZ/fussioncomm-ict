1) Who we are

FussionComm ICT (“we”, “us”, “our”) is a startup ICT company based in Pretoria, South Africa. We provide web development and related services for startups and SMEs.

2) Scope

This policy explains what personal data we collect via this website, how we use it, and the choices you have. If you become a paying client, your project will also be governed by the terms in your service agreement.

3) Data we collect

  • Contact form: name, email address, message content (and the time of submission and IP for anti-abuse).
  • Accounts (signup/login): name, email, username, password (stored as a cryptographic hash), and basic activity such as last login.
  • Server logs: standard web logs (IP address, user-agent, pages viewed, timestamps) for security and troubleshooting.
  • Cookies: an authentication/session cookie (fc_session) if you sign in; basic preferences where applicable. We do not use advertising cookies.

4) How and why we use your data

  • To respond to inquiries you send via the contact form.
  • To provide access to the admin area for authorized users and maintain account security.
  • To protect the service, prevent abuse, and diagnose issues (security monitoring and logging).
  • To improve website usability and content (aggregate, non-identifying insights).

5) Legal bases

  • Contract — to provide requested services or respond to your request for a quote.
  • Legitimate interests — security, fraud prevention, site performance.
  • Consent — where required (e.g., if we ever introduce optional analytics or marketing communications).

6) Retention

  • Contact messages: typically up to 12 months unless a longer period is needed for a potential engagement or legal reasons.
  • Account data: for as long as the account is active; some records may remain in backups for a limited period.
  • Server logs: generally up to 30 days; security events may be kept longer.
  • Backups: encrypted backups may be retained up to 90 days.

7) Sharing & transfers

We do not sell your data. We may share limited data with trusted providers strictly to run this website (e.g., hosting, email/SMS). Those providers act under contracts and equivalent safeguards. Data may be processed in South Africa and, where relevant, other jurisdictions with appropriate protections.

8) Security

We use industry-standard measures (encryption in transit, hashed passwords, access controls). No method is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or misuse.

9) Your rights

Subject to applicable law (including GDPR where relevant), you may request to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you;
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data;
  • Delete your data (“right to be forgotten”);
  • Restrict or object to certain processing;
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format.

To exercise your rights, contact us at fussioncomm@proton.me. We may need to verify your identity before acting on requests.

10) Children

Our site and services are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided data, contact us to remove it.
